Customer Journey Deep Dive

Traced where customers were getting lost (spoiler: it was step 3). Found the drop-off points and suggested fixes that didn't require a complete rebuild.

Overview

The marketing team knew customers were dropping off somewhere in their journey, but couldn't pinpoint exactly where or why. They needed a clear picture of the customer experience to identify improvement opportunities.

Solution

I mapped the entire customer journey using data from multiple touchpoints, creating visualizations that showed exactly where customers were getting stuck. The analysis revealed that step 3 in their onboarding process had a 40% drop-off rate.

  • End-to-end journey mapping with conversion rates
  • Drop-off analysis at each stage
  • Customer segmentation by behavior patterns
  • Actionable recommendations for quick wins

Results

The team implemented targeted fixes at the identified drop-off points, focusing on step 3 first. The changes were simple‚Äîbetter messaging and clearer instructions‚Äîbut they didn't require a complete rebuild. Conversion rates improved significantly within weeks.
