Quick Data Fix

They needed answers yesterday. I built a quick analysis tool in a day that answered their burning questions and saved them from making a bad decision.

Overview

A client had a critical decision to make and needed data analysis fast. They were considering a major investment but didn't have the insights to know if it was the right move. Time was running out.

Solution

Instead of proposing a month-long analysis, I built a focused analysis tool in a day that answered their specific questions. I pulled the relevant data, created clear visualizations, and delivered actionable insights they could use immediately.

  • Rapid data extraction and cleaning
  • Focused analysis on key decision factors
  • Clear visualizations showing risks and opportunities
  • Immediate recommendations based on findings

Results

The client got the answers they needed in time to make an informed decision. The analysis revealed that the investment wasn't the right move at that moment, saving them from a costly mistake. They appreciated the speed and clarity of the insights.
