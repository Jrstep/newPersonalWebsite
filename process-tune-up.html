Process Tune-Up

Looked at how things were actually getting done (not how they were supposed to), found the bottlenecks, and cut processing time by 40%. The team was thrilled.

Overview

The operations team was frustrated with slow processing times, but the official process documentation didn't match reality. They needed someone to figure out what was actually happening and find the bottlenecks.

Solution

I analyzed the actual workflow by tracking time spent at each stage and identifying where delays were occurring. The data revealed several bottlenecks that weren't in the official process docs—manual handoffs, redundant approvals, and data entry errors that required rework.

  • Workflow analysis using actual time-tracking data
  • Bottleneck identification and prioritization
  • Streamlined approval processes
  • Automated data validation to reduce errors

Results

Processing time dropped by 40% after implementing the recommended changes. The team was thrilled because the improvements were practical and didn't require major system overhauls—just smarter workflows based on what was actually happening.
