Report Automation

Automated a monthly report that used to take 8 hours. Now it runs itself and the team gets their data on time, every time. They were pretty happy about that.

Overview

The team was spending 8 hours every month manually compiling a report that was always the same format. It was tedious, error-prone, and took valuable time away from actual analysis work.

Solution

I automated the entire report generation process, creating a script that pulls data from multiple sources, performs the necessary calculations, formats everything correctly, and delivers the report automatically on schedule.

  • Automated data extraction from multiple sources
  • Automated calculations and formatting
  • Scheduled delivery to stakeholders
  • Error handling and notification system

Results

The report now runs automatically every month without any manual intervention. The team gets their data on time, every time, and those 8 hours are now free for more valuable work. They were pretty happy about that‚Äîand so was their manager.
